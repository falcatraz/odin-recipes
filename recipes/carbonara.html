Carbonara

Carbonara is a very simple, yet delicious Italian dish that is sure to give you a satisfied belly once you are done with it. It is easy to make, easy to buy since it is affordable, and easy to enjoy! Below are the ingredients and directions to make this delicious dish

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Eggs
  • Cheese
  • Bacon
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Directions/Steps

  1. Make the pasta
  2. Drain the water, keep some of it
  3. Mix the egg, cheese, salt, pepper, and bacon together
  4. Pour yourself some wine
  5. Enjoy!
